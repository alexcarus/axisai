# AXIS Wallet [One wallet — mine, bridge, send &amp; receive]

Your **self-custodial** AXIS wallet. Unlock once, then bridge, send and receive
across **Base** and **Robinhood Chain** — no browser extension needed, so it
works right inside the Telegram Mini App. Your key is encrypted with your
password and never leaves your device.

## Notes

- **Self-custodial.** The encrypted key lives only on this device; AXIS never
  sees it. Sweep large holdings to a hardware wallet.
- **Same address on both chains.** Your AXIS address is identical on Base and
  Robinhood Chain — receive on either.
- **Bridging** locks AXIS on Base and mints 1:1 on Robinhood (and burns back),
  via the ownerless LayerZero OFT bridge. See the [Bridge](/bridge) page for details.
