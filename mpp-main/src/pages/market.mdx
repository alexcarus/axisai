# AXIS Market [Trade with AI — traders and miners both earn]

The AXIS Market turns the network's verified AI computation into a trading
engine. A trader submits an order and receives an **AI-optimized quote**; the
same Proof-of-AI-Work miners that secure the network also price and route the
order. Every fill pays a small protocol fee that **splits between the liquidity
pool and the miners** — so liquidity providers earn yield, miners earn for their
compute, and traders get tighter pricing.

## How a trade works

1. **Submit an order** — choose a side (buy/sell) and size in AXIS.
2. **Receive an AI quote** — the engine returns a price with an AI-tightened
   spread, the notional, and the fee.
3. **Fee split** — the protocol fee on the fill is divided:

```
fee = notional × 0.50%
  ├─ 60% → liquidity providers   (the capital that fills the order)
  └─ 40% → AXIS AI miners         (the inference that prices + routes it)
```

4. **Settlement** — the order settles against the liquidity pool; the miner
   share is credited to the AI compute that produced the quote.

## AI auto-trade — let AXIS AI trade for you

Toggle **“Let AXIS AI trade for me”** and the engine trades on your behalf. The
AI sizes and times each order using the network's inference; the **trader earns
the optimized PnL**, and the **miners earn the fee on every cycle**. It is the
same Proof-of-AI-Work loop as mining — only here the AI work is *trading*, and
both sides of the market are rewarded:

- **Traders earn** — AI-optimized execution and auto-trading PnL.
- **Miners earn** — the AI/compute share of every fee, on top of block rewards.
- **Liquidity earns** — yield on the capital that fills orders.

## Submit and receive a quote programmatically

Institutional traders and bots can request quotes and settle through the market
gateway:

```bash
# Request an AI quote
curl -X POST "$MARKET_URL/market/quote" \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  -d '{"side":"buy","asset":"AXIS","amount":"250"}'

# -> { price, notional, fee, split: { liquidity, miner }, quote_id, expires_at }

# Accept the quote to settle (splits liquidity + miner automatically)
curl -X POST "$MARKET_URL/market/execute" \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  -d '{"quote_id":"q_…"}'
```
